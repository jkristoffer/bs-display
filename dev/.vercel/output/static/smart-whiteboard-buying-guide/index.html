What is a Smart Whiteboard?

A smart whiteboard is a digital interactive screen that enables collaboration, real-time annotations, and presentations, effectively replacing traditional whiteboards. These modern displays combine touch technology with high-resolution screens to create versatile tools for education, business, and creative environments.

Why Choose Interactive Whiteboards?

Ideal for Remote Work

Perfect for hybrid and remote setups with digital sharing capabilities and cloud integration.

FAQs

Do smartboards work with gloves?

It depends on the touch technology. Infrared (IR) and optical systems work with gloves, while capacitive screens typically don't unless they're specially designed for capacitive touch.

Which technology is best for artists?

Electromagnetic Resonance (EMR) technology paired with OLED displays offers the highest precision and pressure sensitivity, making it ideal for artists and designers who need detailed control.

What's the lifespan of a smartboard?

Most quality smart whiteboards have a lifespan of 5-7 years with regular use. OLED displays may have shorter lifespans (3-5 years) while LED displays can last 7-10 years with proper care.

Is a special pen always required?

No. IR, optical, and resistive technologies work with any object. EMR requires a special pen, while capacitive screens work with fingers and compatible styluses.
